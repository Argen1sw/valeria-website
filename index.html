About Us

We are a family-owned business based in Richmond, Virginia, and we pride ourselves on providing top-quality handyman services to our community. Our team of skilled professionals has years of experience in the industry, and we are dedicated to providing you with exceptional service and workmanship. We believe in doing the job right the first time, and we always go above and beyond to ensure your complete satisfaction.

At RVA Handyman, we offer a wide range of services, including general repairs, carpentry, electrical work, plumbing, painting, and much more. We are fully licensed and insured, and we use only the highest-quality materials and tools to ensure your project is completed to the highest standards.
